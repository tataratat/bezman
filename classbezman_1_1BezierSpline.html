◆ DerivativeWRTParametricDimension()

constexpr BezierSpline DerivativeWRTParametricDimension ( const std::array< IndexingType, parametric_dimension > &  orders) const
constexpr

Determine the close form representation of the derivative

The orders are set per parameteric dimension, meaning that {1, 2} will result in the first order derivative along the first parametric axis and the second order derivative alon the second parametric axis.

The control points are computed as follows

\[ \hat{C}_i^k=\frac{p!}{(p-k)!}\sum_{j=0}^k (-1)^{j+1}\binom{k}{j}C_{i+j} \]

where \(\hat{C}_i^k\) denotes the control point of the close form of spline the derivative, \( p \) is the degree of the original spline, \(k\) is the order of the derivative (in a given parametric direction) \(C_i\) are the original control points

Parameters
ordersarray denoting the orders along the respective parametric axis
